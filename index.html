In the following production, I present a collection of people — researchers and participants — in a clinical study of a hypothetical drug. During this process, something goes wrong for people of AANHPI (Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander) heritage, and the researchers must respond accordingly. It is depicted through an artistic lens and in a style that will be familiar to many young people today.
